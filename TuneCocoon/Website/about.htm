Lend us your ears and we will take you through the digital revolution.

Tune Cocoon, LLC began in Albuquerque when its founders needed to convert their enormous CD collections to the new digital formats. Realizing the countless hours they would lose in their otherwise busy lives, they decided to produce a large scale solution to rip the music in hours not months — basically they are music and technology nerds.

 Once they solved the problem, it seemed only natural to share the solution with you. Key to our client focused philosophy is spending time with each client to ensure that their metamorphosis to digital music is seamless. After all, you want to spend your time enjoying your music, not converting it.
