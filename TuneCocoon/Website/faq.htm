What does Tune Cocoon do?
See our Services and Pricing page for the best answer.

What does the service cost?
The basic service charge is $1.25 per CD we rip. More services are found on our Services and Pricing page.

How long does the conversion take?
Depends upon the size of your collection. Generally, you will receive your CD’s in a week or less (plus shipping).

How does pick-up and delivery work?
If you live in Albuquerque, NM, then a member of our team will arrive at your house, with proper ID, and review your collection and order. Plan to spend 30 minutes with our representative.

What audio formats are available?
Our standard audio conversion is to 192kbps MP3, the most universally playable high-quality format. However, virtually every other format is available for a small additional charge (prices vary by format).

How much hard drive room will I need for my CD collection?
There are many variables that affect this answer. In general, every 100 audio CDs will need about 7 GB of hard drive space when converted to 192kbps MP3 format.

Why are you returning my music on DVDs?
So that you have a permanent backup of your entire music collection on just a few discs, which we give to you in a hard protective case. A DVD holds about 65 converted audio CDs.

What if I do not have a DVD drive in my computer?
First, we recommend you get one and we will be happy to add that to our service. If not, we also offer backup of your digital music library onto CDs that can be read by a computer for the additional charge of $1 per backup disc. Generally, it will take 6.5 CDs to replace every DVD.

What about a hard drive backup?
We HIGHLY RECOMMEND A HARD DRIVE BACKUP, and not just because Tune Cocoon sells them—we will be happy to load the external hard drive you already own. Putting your collection on a hard drive offers mobility, added security, and relieves the burden on your home computer.

My CD collection is a little scratched; will Tune Cocoon be able to convert them?
Tune Cocoon makes every effort to convert your CDs, even if they are not in pristine condition. If your CD cannot be converted, Tune Cocoon can attempt to fix the scratches for an additional charge.

Are there any types of CDs that won't be ripped?
Tune Cocoon will not rip copy protected, badly scratched, pirated or copied CDs.
